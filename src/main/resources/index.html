Nostalgia Games Arcade

Here are some of the games I made back in high school. They're all Java Applets (ick!) but should still run on the latest JRE.

  • Street Rider Play

    The object of the game is to score as many points as you can. You earn points by hopping over obstacles. Small Ramps jump you 1 space, while big ramps jump you 3 spaces.

  • Dirt Racer Play

    Top-down scrolling game; dodge the obstacles and beat the times.

  • Punish the Poser Play

    Throw rocks at the "poser".

  • X-Up! Play

    Our most ambitious project; you perform tricks by entering key-combos in the air. We posed a GI-Joe action figure on a finger bike to get the graphics for it.

  • Whack-a-mole Play

    For whatever reason, I used a really creepy mole picture for this one.

  • Breakout Play

    It's the classic ball-breaker game.
